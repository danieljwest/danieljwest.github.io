# Where to Find the Sheet Music

This page gives **specific books and publishers** for the pieces in the practice plan so you know which version to look for. Always get **B♭ trumpet** parts unless you play a different key. Many titles are available from **Hal Leonard**, **Sheet Music Plus**, **JW Pepper**, **Amazon**, or your local music store.

## Method books (Phase 1 — simple tunes and bridges)

These books contain simple melodies like "Ode to Joy," "Long Long Ago," "Beautiful Dreamer," "Greensleeves," and other common tunes. Page and piece order vary by edition; use the table of contents to find the piece you need.

| Book | Publisher | What you get | Where to buy |
|------|-----------|--------------|--------------|
| **Standard of Excellence, Book 1** (and Book 2) | **Kjos** (Neil A. Kjos Music) | Concert pieces and solos in progressive order; many simple tunes (Ode to Joy–type) and slightly harder pieces. | [Kjos](https://kjos.com), Sheet Music Plus, JW Pepper, music stores |
| **Essential Elements for Band, Book 1** (and Book 2) | **Hal Leonard** | Similar progression; simple tunes and concert pieces for trumpet. | [Hal Leonard](https://www.halleonard.com), Sheet Music Plus, JW Pepper, music stores |

**Note:** "Ode to Joy," "Mary Had a Little Lamb," "Long Long Ago," "Beautiful Dreamer," "Greensleeves," "Aura Lee," and "Theme from Brahms" (or similar) often appear in one or both of these method books. If a piece isn’t in your book, search the piece name + "trumpet Bb" on Sheet Music Plus or Musicnotes for a single-sheet or digital copy.

## Phase 1 — First piece and bridge pieces

### First Book of Trumpet Solos

| Item | Publisher | Contents | Where to buy |
|------|-----------|----------|--------------|
| **First Book of Trumpet Solos** (trumpet + piano) | **Faber Music** | Edited by John Wallace & John Miller. Pieces in order of difficulty from first lessons up to about grade 4. Good for Piece 1 and bridge pieces (e.g. Lullaby, then next pieces in the book). | [Faber](https://www.fabermusic.com), Sheet Music Plus, Amazon. ISBN: 9780571508464 |

Use the **next piece in the book** after the one you chose for Piece 1 as your bridge; the book is designed to progress in small steps.

## Phase 2 — Vandercook solos (Vega, Lyra, Mira, etc.)

### Trumpet Stars (Vandercook) — Rubank / Hal Leonard

All are **B♭ trumpet with piano**. Available as book/CD packs.

| Book | Publisher | Pieces in this book | Where to buy |
|------|-----------|---------------------|--------------|
| **Trumpet Stars, Set 1** | **Rubank (Hal Leonard)** | Lyra, Vega, Cygnus, Antares, Altair, Arcturus | [Hal Leonard](https://www.halleonard.com) (search "Trumpet Stars Set 1"), Sheet Music Plus, JW Pepper |
| **Trumpet Stars, Set 2** | **Rubank (Hal Leonard)** | Spica, Centaurus, Orion, Sirius, **Mira**, **Rigel** | Same as above; search "Trumpet Stars Set 2" |

- **Phase 2 Piece 2A:** Use **Set 1** for **Vega** and **Lyra** (and **Arcturus** as an alternative).
- **Phase 2 Piece 2B:** Use **Set 2** for **Mira** (and **Centaurus** as an alternative).
- **Phase 2 Piece 2C:** **Trumpeter’s Lullaby** is not in Trumpet Stars; see below.
- **Phase 2 Piece 2D (optional):** Use **Set 2** for **Rigel**.

## Phase 4 — Il Triello

| Item | Publisher | Format | Where to buy |
|------|------------|--------|--------------|
| **Il Triello** (The Good, the Bad and the Ugly) — trumpet solo with band or piano | **Molenaar Edition** (arr. **Johan de Meij**) | Trumpet solo + concert band or piano reduction. | Search "Il Triello trumpet Johan de Meij" or "Il Triello Molenaar trumpet." Available from **Stretta Music**, **Molenaar**, and other band music retailers. |

Other arrangements of Il Triello (different publishers) exist; the **Johan de Meij** arrangement for trumpet and band is the one commonly used for solo/band performance.

---

**If a piece isn’t in the book you have:** Search **"[piece name] trumpet Bb"** or **"[piece name] trumpet sheet music"** on **Sheet Music Plus**, **Musicnotes**, or **JW Pepper** to find a correct B♭ trumpet edition. Prefer editions that say "B♭ trumpet" or "trumpet in B♭" so the part matches your instrument.
